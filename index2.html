Welcome to Anchored Abroad

A travel blog featuring budget-friendly travel advice, essential packing tips and thoughtful insights from around the globe.

Over the past two years, I’ve had the incredible experience of traveling to 10 countries – all while sticking to a budget. From the bustling streets of Bangkok to the vibrant markets of Morocco, I’ve learned how to make the most of every destination without breaking the bank. On Anchored Abroad, I share not only my personal travel stories but also budget-friendly travel tips, packing advice, and cultural insights from around the globe. Whether you're planning a solo adventure or exploring with friends, you'll find practical advice and inspiration to help you embark on your own affordable journey. Join me as I continue to explore the world, find new roots, and show you how every adventure can feel like home – without the hefty price tag.

Discover the world, travel smart, and make every journey an unforgettable one.
